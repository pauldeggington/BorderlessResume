Our Story

Why I built DesiCV

Finding work is hard enough. Having your application silently rejected because of how your CV is formatted, before anyone has even read it, does not feel fair. Because it isn't. I have been there. And so have people close to me.

Watching friends and colleagues, genuinely talented people, get passed over not because of what they had done, but because their experience was formatted for the wrong market. A degree abbreviation a recruiter did not recognise. An objective statement that read as outdated. A project table that confused an ATS system before a human ever saw it.

The knowledge of what each country actually wants, what each industry expects, what gets you through the door versus what gets you filtered out, that knowledge exists. It has never been built into a tool designed for us, until now.

Western resume builders were built for Western job seekers. South Asian professionals have been making do ever since.

DesiCV is the tool I wished existed. Built around the actual requirements of each country, each industry, each hiring system, so that the work you have done speaks for itself, wherever you are applying.
